About Us

Our story, mission, and commitment to excellence in geospatial surveying

Our Journey

Founded in 2010, Geospring Geosystem Surveyors began with a clear vision: to revolutionize land surveying in Kenya through technological innovation and uncompromising integrity.

What started as a small team of three passionate surveyors operating from a modest office in Bungoma has grown into a respected geospatial solutions provider serving clients across multiple counties.

Our Mission

To deliver precise, reliable, and timely geospatial solutions that empower sustainable land development across Kenya.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted partner for innovative geospatial solutions and land consultancy services.
